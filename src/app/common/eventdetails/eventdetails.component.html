28th Oct 2022

The grand opening ceremony of the Vikas Deepotsav Mela organized by the Municipal Corporation was completed at Ramlila Ground Linepar Bhubaneswar, in which the State Panchayati Raj Minister Mr. X X Chaudhary, who was present as the chief guest, said in his address that the fair is integral to our Ganga Jamuni culture and communal harmony. It is a part and such events teach to live in harmony with each other. He said that fairs remove the tension prevailing in the public mind and give the message of mutual love.

Khordha, India
